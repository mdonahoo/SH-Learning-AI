## Personal Development Plan

### Priority 1: Keep Learning and Growing
**Action Steps:**
- After each mission, ask yourself "What did I learn?" and "What would I do differently?"
- Share one thing you learned with the team at the end of every mission.
- **Practice:** Keep a 'lessons learned' journal - write down one new thing after each activity.

### Priority 2: Create Better Solutions Together
**Action Steps:**
- When facing a challenge, ask teammates for their ideas. Build on others' suggestions using "Yes, and..."
- Ask the team, "Does anyone have ideas?" before making decisions alone.
- **Practice:** Combine at least two people's ideas into your solution during group projects.

### Priority 3: Prioritize What Matters
**Action Steps:**
- Identify what is most important when multiple things need attention. Do urgent and hard tasks first.
- Verbally identify the most critical alert when multiple alerts occur.
- **Practice:** Make a to-do list and mark the top three most important items.

## What To Practice Next

### Quick Wins (Try These Now!)

**1. Communication Clarity Drill**
- Before speaking, form a complete sentence in your mind.
- *Scout Connection:* A Scout is Trustworthy - clear communication builds trust
- *7 Habits:* Habit 5: Seek First to Understand - clarity enables understanding

**2. Voice Projection Training**
- Practice speaking clearly and confidently using the 'Radio Voice' technique.
- *Scout Connection:* A Scout is Brave - confidence in communication
- *7 Habits:* Habit 1: Be Proactive - take ownership of being understood

**3. Closed-Loop Communication Protocol**
- Implement three-part acknowledgments: Repeat, Confirm Understanding, Report Completion.
- *Scout Connection:* A Scout is Obedient - following and confirming orders
- *7 Habits:* Habit 5: Seek First to Understand, Then to Be Understood

### Team Drills

**Rapid Fire Communications** (10 minutes)
- Leader reads scenario card.
- Target station must respond within 5 seconds with acknowledgment and action.
- Evaluate clarity.

*Debrief Questions:*
- Which responses were clearest? Why?
- What made some responses difficult?

**Station Swap Challenge** (20 minutes)
- Original operator teaches partner the basics for 5 minutes.
- Partners swap stations and run simple scenarios.
- Original operators can coach but not take over.

*Debrief Questions:*
- What was harder than expected about the other role?
- What did you learn that will help you support that station?
